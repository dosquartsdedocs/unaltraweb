Die zentralen Orte in Suddeutschland

Student: Walter Christaller

Status: Defended

Institution: University of Erlangen

Year: 1933

Foundational doctoral work in urban and economic geography. It formalized central place theory and explained hierarchical settlement patterns and service areas.

Innovationsforloppet ur Korologisk Synpunkt (innovation diffusion as a spatial process)

Student: Torsten Hagerstrand

Status: Defended

Institution: Lund University

Year: 1953

Landmark doctoral research on spatial diffusion. It modeled how innovations spread across places through distance, contact networks, and temporal stages.

Agricultural and rural change in Kent (1800-1900)

Student: David Harvey

Status: Defended

Institution: University of Cambridge

Year: 1961

Early doctoral research in historical and regional geography. It analyzed long-term agrarian transformation and rural restructuring, anticipating later critical approaches in geography.
